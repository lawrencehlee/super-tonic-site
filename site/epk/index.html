Bio

Super Tonic is a San Diego-based anime and video game cover band bringing their favorite songs to life with full 9-piece arrangements and energetic live performances. The band is a mainstay in the Southern California scene for anime and video game fans, having played at events like Animé Los Angeles, Brew Game Plus, and the Cherry Blossom Festival at the Japanese Frienship Garden & Museum.

Super Tonic was born in 2019 from a combination of alumni from two of UC San Diego's musical ensembles, The Intermission Orchestra and Pepband. From the start, they envisioned a full band to capture the magic of the songs in the space: lush vocal harmonies (Lauren, Sarah), a tight horn section (Andrei, Ian), and a solid rhythm section behind it all (Zi, Alyssa, Kevin, and Lawrence).

In 2025, they released their debut EP, Phoenix Down, with renditions of some of the most beloved songs in their repertoire.
